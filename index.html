    Nail Health Information

    What Nails Tell Us

    Your nails can reveal important information about your overall health and nutritional status. Changes in color, texture, or shape may indicate deficiencies or health conditions.

    Common Deficiencies

    Brittle nails may suggest iron deficiency, white spots could indicate zinc deficiency, and spoon-shaped nails might point to iron-deficiency anemia.

    Nail Care Tips

    Keep nails clean and dry, avoid harsh chemicals, moisturize regularly, and maintain a balanced diet rich in biotin, protein, and minerals.
